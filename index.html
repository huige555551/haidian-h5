Haidian Introduction

Haidian District, located in northwestern Beijing, and one of Beijing’s 16 districts and counties, covers an area of 430.8 km2, which is 2.53% of Beijing’s total area. Haidian District is a national base for high-tech industries, a world-renowned habitat for higher education & scientific research institutes, and a national famous destination for tourism and cultural & sports activities.

Haidian District is divided into four functional zones: science, education and culture industry zone in the east, new urban zone in the north, modern service industry zone in the south, and eco-friendly & recreational tourism zone in the west.

As the core of Zhongguancun National Innovation Demonstration Park, Haidian District will emphasize science & technology innovation in future development, make full use of the competitive innovation resources of Zhongguancun, and prioritize industries of electronic information, bio-pharmacy, aviation & aerospace, advanced material, clean energy, modern agriculture, advanced manufacture, energy conservation and emission reduction, etc.
